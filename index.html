Dataset Features

Comprehensive meteorological data for weather forecasting and climate analysis

Temperature Data

Accurate temperature readings in Celsius and Fahrenheit, including feels-like temperature and dew point measurements.

Humidity & Precipitation

Relative humidity percentages and precipitation data with probability forecasts for weather prediction models.

Wind Patterns

Wind speed, direction, and gust data for comprehensive atmospheric analysis and forecasting.

Atmospheric Pressure

Barometric pressure readings in hectopascals for weather system tracking and prediction.

Multiple Locations

Weather data from 5 major Indian cities with geographic coordinates for spatial analysis.

Time Series Format

Hourly and 3-hourly observations perfect for time series analysis and machine learning models.
